## 1. Overview of Fixed Income Funds

Fixed income funds invest in bonds and debt instruments, providing regular income and capital preservation. They are generally suitable for all risk profiles, with allocation levels varying based on client risk tolerance. Fixed income instruments typically exhibit lower volatility than equities but offer more modest long-term returns.

## 2. Available Fixed Income Products

### 2.1 Core Bond Fund (CBF)
- **Benchmark**: Bloomberg U.S. Aggregate Bond Index
- **Strategy**: Broad investment-grade bond exposure (government + corporate)
- **Expense Ratio**: 0.04%
- **Minimum Investment**: $5,000
- **Risk Level**: Low-Moderate
- **Suitable Profiles**: All profiles
- **Duration**: ~6 years (moderate interest rate sensitivity)
- **3-Year Annualized Return**: -1.8%
- **Current Yield**: 4.7%

### 2.2 Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities Fund (TIPSF)
- **Benchmark**: Bloomberg U.S. TIPS Index
- **Strategy**: U.S. government TIPS, inflation-linked returns
- **Expense Ratio**: 0.05%
- **Minimum Investment**: $5,000
- **Risk Level**: Low
- **Suitable Profiles**: All profiles (especially conservative)
- **Inflation Hedge**: Yes — principal adjusts with CPI
- **3-Year Annualized Return**: 0.5%
- **Note**: Excellent hedge for conservative clients concerned about inflation eroding purchasing power.

### 2.3 High Yield Corporate Bond Fund (HYCBF)
- **Benchmark**: ICE BofA U.S. High Yield Index
- **Strategy**: Sub-investment grade corporate bonds ("junk bonds")
- **Expense Ratio**: 0.50%
- **Minimum Investment**: $15,000
- **Risk Level**: High (credit risk)
- **Suitable Profiles**: Growth, Aggressive only
- **3-Year Annualized Return**: 4.2%
- **Current Yield**: 8.1%
- **Warning**: Significant default risk. Not suitable for Conservative or Balanced clients.

### 2.4 Short-Duration Bond Fund (SDBF)
- **Benchmark**: ICE BofA 1-3 Year U.S. Treasury Index
- **Strategy**: Short maturity government and high-grade corporate bonds
- **Expense Ratio**: 0.07%
- **Minimum Investment**: $5,000
- **Risk Level**: Low
- **Suitable Profiles**: All profiles (capital preservation, liquidity)
- **Duration**: ~1.8 years (low interest rate sensitivity)
- **3-Year Annualized Return**: 1.2%
- **Note**: Preferred for clients with near-term liquidity needs (1-3 years).

## 3. Interest Rate Risk

Fixed income securities are inversely correlated with interest rates. When rates rise, bond prices fall. Longer-duration funds carry greater interest rate risk.

| Fund | Duration | Rate Sensitivity |
|---|---|---|
| Core Bond Fund | ~6 yrs | Moderate |
| TIPS Fund | ~7 yrs | Moderate (partially hedged via inflation link) |
| High Yield Fund | ~4 yrs | Moderate-High (plus credit risk) |
| Short-Duration Fund | ~1.8 yrs | Low |

## 5. Credit Quality Standards

All fixed income investments must comply with credit quality minimums:
- Conservative clients: Investment grade only (BBB- or higher)
- Balanced clients: Primarily investment grade; ≤10% high yield
- Growth clients: Up to 20% high yield permitted
- Aggressive clients: Up to 40% high yield permitted
