## 1. Overview of Alternative Investments

Alternative investments include asset classes and strategies outside traditional equities and fixed income. They offer diversification benefits, low correlation to public markets, and potential for enhanced returns. However, they typically carry higher complexity, lower liquidity, and require higher minimum investments.

**Access**: This guide is for internal RM use only. Alternative investment products should only be presented to clients who meet minimum AUM thresholds and appropriate risk profile requirements.

---

## 2. Available Alternative Investment Products

### 2.1 Real Estate Investment Trust Fund (REITF)
- **Type**: Listed REITs (liquid)
- **Strategy**: Diversified real estate exposure (commercial, residential, industrial)
- **Expense Ratio**: 0.12%
- **Minimum Investment**: $10,000
- **Risk Level**: Moderate-High
- **Suitable Profiles**: Balanced, Growth, Aggressive
- **Liquidity**: Daily (listed ETF)
- **3-Year Annualized Return**: 5.2%
- **Yield**: 3.8% dividend yield
- **Minimum AUM**: $500,000

### 2.2 Global Macro Alternatives Fund (GMAF)
- **Type**: Active alternative strategy fund
- **Strategy**: Long/short across equities, fixed income, FX, commodities
- **Expense Ratio**: 1.50%
- **Minimum Investment**: $100,000
- **Risk Level**: High
- **Suitable Profiles**: Growth (limited allocation), Aggressive
- **Liquidity**: Monthly redemption with 30-day notice
- **3-Year Annualized Return**: 7.4%
- **Minimum AUM**: $1,000,000
- **Note**: Requires enhanced suitability assessment. Not suitable for clients with short investment horizons.

### 2.3 Private Credit Fund (PCF)
- **Type**: Private / illiquid
- **Strategy**: Direct lending to mid-market companies
- **Expense Ratio**: 1.00% management + 20% performance fee
- **Minimum Investment**: $250,000
- **Risk Level**: High (credit + illiquidity risk)
- **Suitable Profiles**: Aggressive only
- **Liquidity**: 3-year lock-up, then quarterly redemption
- **Target Return**: 10-12% p.a. (net of fees)
- **Minimum AUM**: $2,000,000
- **Warning**: Requires licensed financial advisor sign-off. Cannot be recommended by RM alone.

---

## 3. Suitability Considerations for Alternatives

- Alternatives are **not suitable for Conservative clients** under any circumstances.
- Balanced clients may access REIT funds with allocation ≤15%.
- The Private Credit Fund requires **licensed advisor approval** — RMs must escalate.
- All alternatives require client acknowledgement of illiquidity risk (where applicable).
