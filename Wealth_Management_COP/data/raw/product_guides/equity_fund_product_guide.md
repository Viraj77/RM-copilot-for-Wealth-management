## 1. Overview of Equity Funds

Equity funds invest primarily in company stocks with the objective of capital growth over the medium to long term. They are suitable for investors with a balanced, growth, or aggressive risk profile and an investment horizon of at least 5 years.

Equity funds carry higher short-term volatility compared to fixed income instruments, but historically deliver superior long-term returns.

---

## 2. Available Equity Fund Products

### 2.1 Broad Market Equity Fund (BMEF)
- **Benchmark**: S&P 500 / MSCI World
- **Strategy**: Passive index tracking
- **Expense Ratio**: 0.05%
- **Minimum Investment**: $10,000
- **Risk Level**: Moderate-High
- **Suitable Profiles**: Balanced, Growth, Aggressive
- **3-Year Annualized Return**: 11.2%

### 2.2 Technology Growth Fund (TGF)
- **Benchmark**: Nasdaq-100
- **Strategy**: Active management, concentrated tech holdings
- **Expense Ratio**: 0.65%
- **Minimum Investment**: $25,000
- **Risk Level**: High
- **Suitable Profiles**: Growth, Aggressive only
- **3-Year Annualized Return**: 18.4%
- **Warning**: High concentration risk. Not suitable for Conservative or Balanced clients.

### 2.3 International Equity Fund (IEF)
- **Benchmark**: MSCI EAFE
- **Strategy**: Passive, developed market international exposure
- **Expense Ratio**: 0.08%
- **Minimum Investment**: $10,000
- **Risk Level**: Moderate-High
- **Suitable Profiles**: Balanced, Growth, Aggressive
- **3-Year Annualized Return**: 7.8%

### 2.4 Dividend Income Equity Fund (DIEF)
- **Benchmark**: MSCI High Dividend Yield Index
- **Strategy**: Dividend-focused equity, lower volatility
- **Expense Ratio**: 0.15%
- **Minimum Investment**: $10,000
- **Risk Level**: Moderate
- **Suitable Profiles**: Conservative (limited allocation ≤20%), Balanced, Growth
- **3-Year Annualized Return**: 8.5%
- **Note**: Suitable for conservative clients as a small equity allocation due to lower volatility and income generation.

---

## 3. Risk Considerations for Equity Products

- **Market Risk**: All equity funds are subject to market fluctuations.
- **Concentration Risk**: Thematic or sector funds (e.g., TGF) carry elevated concentration risk.
- **Currency Risk**: International funds carry FX risk.
- **Liquidity**: All listed equity ETFs offer daily liquidity.

---

## 4. Allocation Guidelines by Risk Profile

| Risk Profile | Max Equity Allocation | Recommended Range |
|---|---|---|
| Conservative | 25% | 10-20% |
| Balanced | 65% | 45-65% |
| Growth | 80% | 65-80% |
| Aggressive | 100% | 80-100% |

---

## 5. Rebalancing Policy

Portfolios should be reviewed and rebalanced at least annually, or when any asset class drifts more than 10 percentage points from its target allocation. RMs must document all rebalancing decisions and obtain client acknowledgement.
