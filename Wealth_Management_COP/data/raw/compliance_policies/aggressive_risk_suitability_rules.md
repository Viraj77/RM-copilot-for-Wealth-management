# Aggressive & Growth Risk Suitability Rules

## Document Information
- **Type**: Policy
- **Sensitivity**: Internal
- **Date**: 2026-01-01
- **Source**: WealthMgmt Compliance & Suitability Division

---

## 1. Definition of Growth and Aggressive Risk Profiles

### Growth Client
- Seeks capital appreciation over the long term (7+ years)
- Can tolerate short-term drawdowns of up to 30%
- Comfortable with equity-heavy portfolios
- Willing to accept higher volatility for higher expected returns

### Aggressive Client
- Seeks maximum capital appreciation
- Investment horizon of 10+ years
- Can tolerate drawdowns exceeding 40%
- Comfortable with concentrated positions, alternatives, and emerging markets

---

## 2. Permitted Investments

### 2.1 Equity Allocation
| Risk Profile | Min Equity | Max Equity |
|---|---|---|
| Growth | 60% | 80% |
| Aggressive | 70% | 100% |

- All equity fund types are permitted (broad market, sector, international, emerging markets)
- Individual stock positions permitted for Aggressive clients (max 10% per position)
- Emerging markets: Up to 25% for Growth, up to 35% for Aggressive

### 2.2 Fixed Income
- Investment grade and high yield permitted for both profiles
- Growth: Up to 20% high yield
- Aggressive: Up to 40% high yield
- Minimum fixed income: 10% for Growth, 0% for Aggressive

### 2.3 Alternatives
- Growth: Up to 25% alternatives; REIT, liquid alternatives permitted
- Aggressive: Up to 40% alternatives; includes private credit (with licensed advisor)
- Private Credit Fund: Aggressive only, requires licensed advisor sign-off

---

## 3. Special Conditions for Aggressive Clients

- **Leverage**: Low-leverage instruments may be considered with explicit client written consent and licensed advisor approval
- **Concentration Risk**: RMs must flag any single position exceeding 15% of portfolio
- **Liquidity**: Minimum 20% must remain liquid (redeemable within 5 business days)
- **Lock-up**: Illiquid investments must not exceed 30% of total portfolio

---

## 4. Crypto and Digital Assets

Cryptocurrency and digital asset products are classified as speculative instruments:
- **Conservative clients**: PROHIBITED
- **Balanced clients**: PROHIBITED
- **Growth clients**: Not currently approved; refer to licensed advisor
- **Aggressive clients**: Maximum 5% of portfolio with licensed advisor approval and written client consent

RMs are NOT authorised to recommend cryptocurrency under any circumstances. Any client inquiry about crypto MUST be escalated to a licensed advisor.

---

## 5. Unsuitable Cross-Profile Recommendations

The following are ALWAYS unsuitable regardless of client request:
- Unregulated investment schemes
- Binary options or similar speculative derivatives
- Products without prospectus or regulatory disclosure
- Investments in sanctioned entities or jurisdictions

---

## 6. Periodic Review Requirements

Growth and Aggressive profiles must be reviewed at least annually. If a client's life circumstances change (e.g., approaching retirement, major financial event), the RM must re-assess the risk profile and potentially downgrade the allocation policy.
