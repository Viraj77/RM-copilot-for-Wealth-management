# General Compliance Framework for Wealth Management

## Document Information
- **Type**: Policy
- **Sensitivity**: Internal
- **Date**: 2026-01-01
- **Source**: WealthMgmt Legal & Compliance Division

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This framework governs the conduct of all Relationship Managers (RMs) in the provision of wealth management services. It applies to all client interactions, recommendations, and communications.

---

## 2. Know Your Client (KYC) Requirements

Before making any recommendation, RMs must verify and document:
- Client identity and source of funds
- Investment objectives and time horizon
- Financial situation (income, assets, liabilities)
- Risk tolerance (completed questionnaire on file)
- Investment knowledge and experience

KYC information must be updated at least annually or upon any material change in client circumstances.

---

## 3. Suitability Assessment Obligation

All recommendations must be suitable for the individual client based on their KYC profile. Suitability is assessed against:
1. **Risk Profile**: The product risk must align with the client's stated risk tolerance
2. **Investment Horizon**: The product's liquidity and time horizon must match client needs
3. **Financial Situation**: The client must be able to bear the financial risk of loss
4. **Concentration**: No single position should create excessive concentration risk

RMs are prohibited from making recommendations that conflict with a client's suitability profile, even if the client explicitly requests the product.

---

## 4. Disclosure Requirements

RMs must disclose to clients:
- All fees and charges (management fees, performance fees, transaction costs)
- Conflicts of interest (e.g., proprietary products)
- Key risks of each recommended product
- The basis for each recommendation

---

## 5. Licensing and Regulated Advice

### 5.1 RM Authorisation Limits
RMs are authorised to:
- Provide general information about products and markets
- Present product options based on client profile
- Execute client instructions within approved product lists
- Generate ClientBrief decision-support documents

RMs are NOT authorised to:
- Provide personalised investment advice that requires a regulated financial advice licence
- Make discretionary portfolio changes without client instruction
- Promise returns or guarantee outcomes
- Recommend products outside the approved product list

### 5.2 Escalation to Licensed Advisors
The following situations REQUIRE escalation to a licensed financial advisor:
- Any bespoke portfolio construction recommendation
- Alternative investment products (private credit, hedge funds)
- Cryptocurrency and digital asset inquiries
- Leveraged product recommendations
- Situations where client profile has changed significantly
- Any client complaint about a previous recommendation

---

## 6. Record Keeping

All client interactions must be documented:
- Meeting notes within 24 hours of each interaction
- All recommendations with suitability rationale
- Client acknowledgements for risk disclosures
- Escalation referrals and outcomes

Records must be retained for a minimum of 7 years.

---

## 7. Conflicts of Interest Policy

RMs must declare and manage conflicts of interest:
- Proprietary product recommendations must be disclosed
- Performance incentives must not drive unsuitable recommendations
- Personal trading in client holdings is prohibited

---

## 8. Disclaimer Requirements

All client-facing communications must include:
- A statement that content is for informational purposes only
- Clarification that past performance is not indicative of future results
- A statement that the content does not constitute personalised investment advice
- Recommendation to seek independent regulated financial advice

The standard disclaimer is: *"This material is prepared for information purposes and is decision-support for Relationship Managers. It does not constitute personalised investment advice. Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results. Clients should seek independent financial advice tailored to their personal circumstances."*
