1. LICENSED ADVICE TRIGGERS
The following require Wealth Planning team and licensed advisor involvement:
- Personalized withdrawal rate recommendations (e.g., 4% rule for specific client)
- Specific buy/sell/hold trade instructions
- Social Security claiming strategies
- Annuity product recommendations

2. AI COPILOT RULES
- AI briefs are decision support only — not automated advice
- Escalation required before client communication when status is Needs Review
- Human-in-the-loop approval mandatory for escalated briefs

3. DOCUMENTATION
All escalations logged with timestamp, RM ID, client ID, and reason.
