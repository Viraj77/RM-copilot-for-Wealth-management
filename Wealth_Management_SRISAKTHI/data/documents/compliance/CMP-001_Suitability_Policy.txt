1. PURPOSE
Defines suitability requirements for all product recommendations to clients.

2. RISK PROFILE MATRIX
Conservative: Max 25% equity; prohibited high-volatility products (PG-003/HAEF)
Balanced: 30-60% equity; max 10% high-volatility satellite
Growth: 50-80% equity; PG-003 allowed as satellite (max 15%)
Aggressive: 70-95% equity; PG-003 suitable as core growth holding

3. LICENSED ADVICE BOUNDARIES
Personalized withdrawal rates, specific trade orders, and tax optimization
strategies require licensed advisor (Series 7/66) sign-off.

4. CITATION REQUIREMENTS
Every recommendation must cite product guide (PG-xxx) and applicable policy (CMP-xxx).

5. ESCALATION
Cross-profile recommendations and blocked products require compliance review.
