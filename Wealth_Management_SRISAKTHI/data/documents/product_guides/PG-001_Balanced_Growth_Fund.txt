1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
The Horizon Balanced Growth Fund (HBGF) targets long-term capital appreciation
with moderate volatility through a 60/40 equity/fixed income strategic allocation.

2. RISK CHARACTERISTICS
Risk Rating: Moderate (4/10)
Max Drawdown (illustrative): -18%
Suitable for Balanced and Growth risk profiles.

3. SUITABILITY GUIDANCE
Recommended: Balanced, Growth
Conditional: Conservative (max 20% satellite allocation)
NOT suitable for: Aggressive clients seeking concentrated growth (use PG-003/HAEF)

4. RM TALKING POINTS
- Core holding for diversified balanced portfolios
- Complements PG-002 (HCIF) for clients transitioning risk profiles
- Reference CMP-003 for risk profiling methodology
